12.1.1.3 Interaction effect of group x date

From the omnibus model, the interaction effect of group x date indicates that there are differences between the in-flight duration groups in terms of their AM jetlag time profiles. More specifically, the groups differ in the way that AM jetlag changes between time points.

Perceived jetlag data was recorded from 2016-03-10 to 2016-03-16, inclusive. There are two levels within the factor inflight_duration_group. To conduct pairwise comparisons of an interaction effect, we need to create subsets that allow us to compare the two in-flight duration groups on each pair of dates. In other words, we create one subset per pair of dates (i.e., 21 possible date-pairs, so 21 subsets). We run 1 comparison test for each subset (i.e., 21 comparison tests), where we are comparing the higher vs.Â lower in-flight duration groups in terms of their change from one date to another. For this part of the analysis, the Bonferroni-adjusted p value = 0.05 / 21 = 0.00238.
